Lionel Messi

Lionel Andrés Messi, born on June 24, 1987, is an Argentine professional footballer. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time. Messi has won numerous awards and accolades throughout his career, including seven Ballon d'Or titles.

At the club level, Messi played for FC Barcelona from 2004 to 2021, winning numerous domestic and international titles, including four UEFA Champions League titles and ten La Liga titles. In 2021, he transferred to Paris Saint-Germain (PSG).

Career Highlights

  • 7-time Ballon d'Or winner
  • 6-time Golden Boot Winner
  • 4-time UEFA Champions League winner
  • 10-time La Liga winner
  • 6-time Copa del Rey winner
  • Argentina national team captain
  • Copa America 2021 winner
  • World Cup 2022 winner
  • 2-time World Cup Best player
